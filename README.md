

# ⚡ Electric Vehicle Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Electric Vehicle (EV) adoption trends using Power BI, Tableau. The objective is to identify market trends, understand customer preferences, compare Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) and Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs), and provide business insights that can help manufacturers, dealerships, and policymakers make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The rapid growth of the electric vehicle market has created a need to understand:

* Which manufacturers dominate the EV market?
* Which vehicle models are most popular?
* Which regions have the highest EV adoption?
* How has EV adoption changed over time?
* What factors influence EV demand?

This project answers these questions through data analysis and interactive dashboards.

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Tesla is the leading electric vehicle manufacturer in the dataset.
* Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) account for the majority of registered EVs.
* EV adoption has increased significantly in recent years.
* A small number of counties contribute a large share of total EV registrations.
* Some manufacturers have high market demand but relatively limited model variety.

---

## 💼 Business Recommendations

* Expand dealership and charging infrastructure in high-demand regions.
* Focus production on high-performing vehicle models.
* Increase marketing efforts in regions with growing EV adoption.
* Improve charging accessibility to encourage wider EV adoption.
* Use regional demand insights for inventory planning and expansion strategies.
